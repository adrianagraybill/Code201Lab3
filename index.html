Now that you took that quiz, here is some more information on me!

Here's a little bio. I came to the US when I was only 3 years old. I have lived in Seattle since then. This city has given me so much and I love it here. Yes, the weather can put me down at times, but I am always grateful to be here.

My teen years I went to Aviation High School, swam for a year round club team, and worked as a lifeguard.

When I graduated I wasn't quite ready for college, I thought I wanted to be a philosophy major but dropped out because I didn't know what I wanted to do, so I just joined the work force. I worked for Alaska a few years, but I always regretted not pushing myself academically.

I have been wasting years of my life trying to figure stuff out, I decided to give school another shot. I went to Highline College and signed up for CS 101, I liked the content but hated the learning style. I already knew so much and felt like I was wasting time. That's when I decided to try Code Fellows.

Now I am 23 years old, one week in, and I already have a great feeling about this! I am so excited!

If you managed to get through that, the rest should be pretty easy :)
